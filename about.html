About

I was born a young chef in the heart of Italy, and from a young age it was clear that I was born to succeed. My mother's own breast milk was drab and tasteless compared to the food I used to create as an infant. In time I decided to travel across Europe and beyond in search of something that could match my own creations.

As I traveled the world in search of recipes, I came to realize that the world was boring and mediocre.

Now then, I have created this site as a way to share my delicious (and sometimes nutritious) recipes with the world.On this web site you are able to find recipes, photos, and watch me (Chef) cook as a perfect example to base your creations on. I hope you find this website engrossing.

I know that you are all eternally grateful.

-Chef
